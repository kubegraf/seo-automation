How the Pipeline Works

Step 1
🔍 Keyword Discovery
Gemini AI discovers 25+ trending keywords in DevOps, Kubernetes, SRE, and AI-ops domains.
Step 2
🔬 Competitor Analysis
Analyzes 8 competitors (Komodor, Rootly, Deductive AI, Harness…) to find keyword gaps and comparison article opportunities.
Step 3
✍️ Content Generation
Generates full 1500–2500 word articles with headings, code snippets, Mermaid diagrams, and internal links.
Step 4
⚡ SEO Optimization
Scores each article (0–100), adds JSON-LD schema markup, enforces heading hierarchy, and optimizes keyword density.
Step 5
🚀 Publishing
Publishes articles as markdown to this GitHub Pages site and updates the SEO dashboard.
Step 6
📊 SEO Analytics
Tracks keyword rankings, estimates organic traffic, and generates weekly recommendations.
